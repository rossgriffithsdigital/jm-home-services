The story

Started in 2024. Built to last.

In August 2024, Jacob Routhier (16) and Mitch Pringle (20) launched JM Home Services with a simple premise: exterior cleaning in their region was dominated by unreliable operators who showed up late, cut corners, and treated customers like just another invoice.

They wanted to build something different — a company where punctuality wasn't a selling point, it was just the minimum. Where every home was treated like it belonged to someone they respected. Where the work itself mattered.

"A great team of young men working their way through university who present as professional, excellent communicators, well organized with all their own equipment."

— Ellen H., JM Home Services customer

They run JM Home Services while in school and university — managing bookings, driving to jobs, and doing the physical work themselves. What started as a local window cleaning operation in Quinte has expanded to cover 14 communities across Eastern Ontario and Niagara.

Every one of their 50+ Google reviews is five stars. They reply personally to each one.
